Welcome to Code-Monkey!

One of Code-Monkey's goals is to offer current or future students information about life at SoftUni and to share fun and interesting stuff about programming. It is a place for everyone who wants to start out as a software engineer and wants to hear the other side of the story - the students' point of view. The site contains programmer humour and is not expected to be understood by someone who is not a "code-monkey".
